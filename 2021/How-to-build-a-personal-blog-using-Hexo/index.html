How to build a personal blog using Hexo?

May 20th 2021
  • documents

Due to struggling with building this blog for more than 10 hours, I finally know which part I got wrong. Thus, I am writing this blog for > people who might encounter the same issue as me.

Best wishes.
PS: I am not particularly familiar with frontend stuff, thus there might be some stupid wording of a technique or so, please just bear with me that this is just a simple tutorial for anyone like me.

Prerequisite

  1. git installed
  2. node.js installed
  3. your domain (e.g. buy it from some domain selling website like Godaddy etc.)
  4. assume your website is built on github pages (i.e. username.github.io)

Step 1: Change the nameservers of your domain

  1. Go to Cloudfare.com, register and choose the free plan
  2. Press the ‘add site’ and add your domain
  3. Cloudfare will check for the DNS setting of your domain
  4. Cloudfare will suggest two nameservers of their own, use those.
  5. Go to the domain manager on the website you bought your domain, change the nameservers to those that Cloudfare provides
  6. Now, you can change your DNS setting in Cloudfare.
  7. Add a CNAME that points ‘www.your_domain.postfix' to ‘username.github.io’, TCL time is on auto
  8. Now, when you go to your domain, it will show a 404 error of github because you haven’t configure the CNAME in github pages yet.
